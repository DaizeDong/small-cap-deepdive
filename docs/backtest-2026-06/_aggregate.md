## Data-quality flag (load-bearing)

One artifact dominates the raw means: **GNOLF (2024 oilsvc), total_return = 999.0** — entry price $0.00001, exit $0.01, a sub-penny yfinance rounding/penny-stock glitch, not a tradeable 999x gain. It is flagged `buy_eligible`. Including it, the buy_eligible mean excess is **+26.3** (nonsense). **All means below are reported ex-artifact** (n drops by 1 in buy_eligible / normal-2024). Medians and win-rates are artifact-immune. This is the "yfinance delisted/penny approximation" caveat made concrete.

## 3) WATCH-vs-AVOID ordering + pooled blowup-avoidance

- **Ordering is inverted vs thesis.** AVOID+abstain pooled (n=28): median excess +0.024, win 53.6%. WATCH (n=409): median excess -0.067, win 42.5%. The "worse" bucket performed *better*. There is no monotone risk gradient.
- **Pooled blowup-avoidance fraction = 3/66 = 4.5%.** Of 66 names that drew down >40% over the 12-mo horizon, only **3** were in AVOID/abstain (TUSK-2021 AVOID, NCSM-2022 AVOID, RGS-2021 abstain). **58/66 (88%) were WATCH**, and **5 were buy_eligible** (VYND-2020, KLXE-2021, RMSL-2022, LIMX-2022, KLXE-2024). The system routes blowups overwhelmingly into WATCH and even into BUY — it provides **effectively zero blowup-avoidance**.

## 6) Verdict

**No measurable de-risk edge on this panel. The de-risk thesis is not supported; if anything it is mildly contradicted.**

- The core prediction — a risk gradient AVOID/abstain << WATCH << BUY — is **inverted**: AVOID had the best median excess and win rate, BUY/WATCH had negative median excess, and blowup-avoidance is 4.5% (88% of blowups sat in WATCH, 5 in BUY).
- The only axis with any positive read is **directional beta in the 2020 V-bottom and a few 2021/2024 winners** — i.e. the names rode the small-cap recovery, which is "not missing V-bottoms," **not** "avoiding blowups." In the 2022 bear and 2023 cohorts the panel underperformed IWM at the median. So the value, such as it is, is upside-capture in recoveries, not downside protection in stress.
- buy_eligible (n=37 ex-artifact) is statistically indistinguishable from WATCH and lags IWM at the median.

**Caveats:**
1. **regbank/oilsvc capped at 100** universe → regbank produced zero names every year, so the entire regional-bank-crisis stress test (2023) is **untested**; the headline stress conclusion rests only on the 2022 bear cohort.
2. **Small BUY n** (~37 over 5 years, ~7/yr) → buy_eligible stats are high-variance; a single name (GNOLF) swung the raw mean by +26.
3. **yfinance delisted/penny approximation** → at least one outright artifact (GNOLF 999x); penny names (VYRE, EESH, GNOLF, DOGP) carry unreliable sub-cent prices that inflate tails in both directions.
4. **Single 12-month horizon, single 06-30 entry/year** → no path/timing robustness; results are sensitive to the one snapshot date.
5. Means are non-robust (multibagger-driven); the **median** is the honest central tendency and it is negative-to-flat vs IWM in every bucket and most regimes.

**Recommendation:** treat the current bucket labels as **not yet validated for de-risking**. Do not rely on AVOID/abstain to avoid blowups or on buy_eligible to beat the benchmark on this evidence. Re-run with (a) a populated regbank universe to actually test the 2023 crisis, (b) artifact/penny-price guards, and (c) multiple entry dates before claiming any edge.
