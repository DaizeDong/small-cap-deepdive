## 5. OVERALL CROSS-SECTOR ROBUSTNESS VERDICT

**ROBUST, ship-grade as a landmine scanner; do NOT yet trust its recall as a complete universe sweep.**

The v0.3.0 pipeline passed full coverage across all 11 GICS sectors plus 3 regression themes: 53/53 usable, 0 deepdive crashes, 0 silent skips, and, critically, **0 false BUYs leaked.** The two mechanical BUYs (SCOR, TUSK) were both genuine data/model artifacts, and both were caught by the adversarial layer. Every artifact-MoS the system could manufacture (SID +378%, DXC +543%, CHGG +579%, RDGT +1691%, MYPS +265%, BATL +986%, IPW +1054%) was neutralized by extreme_mos_review_required. The signals firewall held byte-clean in all 53 runs. The financial-SIC firewall (regbank, mortgage-reit, BDC) and the pre-revenue/foreign-filer abstain paths behaved exactly as designed, the safe error direction (false negative / abstain) dominated; no unsafe direction (false BUY) survived.

**Two reliability caveats temper the verdict.** (1) Both BUY-to-WATCH downgrades depended on the *human* adversarial layer, because neither the cyclical vetoes (TUSK) nor fundamental_decline (SCOR) fired, the degenerate-base hole (backlog #1) is a real path to a false BUY that the mechanical guards miss. (2) **Recall is the weak axis.** ~30 themes have no SIC floor, the recall@gold harness measures the wrong file, and foreign-filer cohorts are structurally un-valuable. Precision (Gate-2 LLM theme-fit) is excellent and load-bearing, it cut keyword over-recall from thousands of raw FTS hits to handfuls of true members across every theme (logistics 8% precision survived; space-economy 4.9%; cybersecurity killed banks/trusts/biotech wholesale). But a quiet true member that doesn't trip the FTS keyword and has no SIC backstop can still be silently missed, and the test cannot currently prove it wasn't.

**Bottom line:** the machine reliably tells you *what NOT to buy and exactly why*, and it does not fabricate opportunities. Close the degenerate-base hole and the SEC-debt-truncation root cause (backlog #1, #2) before the next cycle; wire the missing SIC floors and fix the recall harness (#5, #6) before claiming the universe sweep is complete.
